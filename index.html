Thank you for visiting!

Welcome to the gallery. From here you can take an online digital walk and esplore the three main rooms of the gallery: Music, Pottery, and Art.

Music

In the music room you can find out about current projects, and also link through to a further room; Little Room. Here you can listen to the extensive catalogue represented by Little Room Recordings and Little Room Music.

Pottery

Earthenware and Stoneware pottery is on show in the Pottery room, including gas fired, electric fired sculptures and pots, slab pots, bottles and wood fired raku work.

Art

Chalk pastel sketches, oils, acrylics, Iconography, and pen and ink drawings are on display in the art room.
